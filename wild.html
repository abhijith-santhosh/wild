OUR STORY

In the late 90s, a group of friends who were passionate about the great outdoors started Wildcraft in a garage. It was still early days for the outdoors and the adventure industry in the country. Quality equipment remained a distant dream for most Indians. Spotting the opportunity, they channelled their passion for the outdoors into a company that would make international quality gear accessible to India’s growing outdoor community. Be it outdoor-friendly clothing, ergonomically designed rucksacks or all-terrain footwear, Wildcraft has one mission – to engineer versatile, multi-functional, light-weight, weather-resilient products for the savvy backpacker; that enables people’s exploration of the outdoors.
